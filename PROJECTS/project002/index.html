Petit Gateau

Ingredients:

  • 200 grams of semi-sweet chocolate
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • Half a cup of sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour

Preparation:

  1. Melt 200 grams of semi-sweet chocolate and 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter in a double boiler. Reserve.
  2. In a mixer, add 2 eggs, 2 egg yolks, and half a cup of sugar. Beat until you get a light cream.
  3. Then, add the chocolate mixture, 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour and mix.
  4. Transfer the mixture to buttered and floured molds.
  5. Bake in a preheated oven at 200 degrees Celsius for 6 to 10 minutes.

Now unmold while still hot and serve with vanilla ice cream.
